Salvador DaBot, the portraitist robot

The Portraitist Robot recognizes human faces in its surroundings and extracts relevant characteristics from them.

By using its uncanny artistic talent, it then draws portraits of the participants from the captured images by converting them into vector art and by using inverse kinematics to control the robot's arm.
